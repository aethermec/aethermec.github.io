Alexa Skill Workshop


What will a student get out of this workshop?
  • Fundamentals of Voice Design book.
  • End to End training and support for building first alexa skill.
  • Internship opportunities at VoiceQube for all participants.
  • One live voice application on alexa skill store with full marketing support from vocieqube.
  • Cloud hardware costs covered.
  • Certificate for becoming a full stack voice application developer.

Day 1

  • Introduction to Voice technology
  • Fundamentals of Voice Design
  • User Utterances and intents
  • Devops and AWS
  • Node.js development
  • Dynamodb and Persistence
  • Application configuration and deployment

Day 2
