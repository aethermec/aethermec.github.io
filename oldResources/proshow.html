Proshows

  • Aether presents to you Neeti Palta, India's favorite female stand up comedian. Voted as the best Stand Up Comic at the Oz Fest, Neeti brings to the forefront the unique female perspective. In an otherwise chauvinistic Indian English comedy scene, she presents the female point of view on a variety of subjects from the life of a woman in India, Indian idiosyncrasies, to daily irritants, current affairs, etc.

  • The five-piece band includes a violinist, Keyboard player, Drummer, Guitarist, and a Singer. Threeory are experimental musicians who perform different adaptations of both English and Bollywood songs. They have performed in many elite places like Bottles and Chimneys, JRC, N convention, Hyatt, Westin, etc.

  • DJ Zaeden is of the youngest Indian DJs. He started playing techno music at the age of 14. He is one of the most wanted DJs in the country today. He has a unique style of combining the new age big room sounds with the classical melodic house. Zaeden has conquered the Indian dance music scene and is now representing India globally.
