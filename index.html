About Us

Aether is popularly believed to be the substance that permeates everything between spaces, a singularity which predates the universe and reality. Why Aether?

To hear one demands silence. We build houses but it's the space inside that makes it livable. We shape clay to pots but it's the emptiness inside that holds.

Aether, that which fills everything is the spirit of the annual techno-cultural fest of Mahindra École Centrale. Let us together embark on a journey towards discovering Aether, the space between transcendence and euphoria!
